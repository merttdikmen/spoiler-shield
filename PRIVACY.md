# Privacy Policy - Spoiler Shield

_Last updated: 2026-06-22_

**Spoiler Shield does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any user data.**

- The only data the extension stores is your **toggle preferences** (which sites and which features are hidden). These are saved via `chrome.storage.sync`. They are never sent to us or to any third party. If you have Chrome Sync enabled, Chrome may sync them across your own devices through your Google account; otherwise they stay on this device.
- The extension reads the page **only** on `youtube.com` and `twitch.tv`, and only to hide on-screen duration, progress-bar, chapter, and timestamp UI. It does not read, log, or transmit your browsing history, the videos you watch, your account, or any page content.
- No analytics, no tracking, no remote code, no external network requests.

Because no personal data is processed, there is nothing to access, export, or delete beyond uninstalling the extension (which removes its stored preferences).

Questions: open an issue on the project's repository.
