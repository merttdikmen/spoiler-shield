storage:
Persists the user's on/off and per-site (thumbnails / player / chapters) toggle preferences locally and syncs them across the user's own Chrome profile. No browsing data is read or stored.

Host access to youtube.com / twitch.tv:
Injects the CSS/JS that hides duration, progress-bar, chapter, and timestamp UI on the two sites this extension supports. It runs only on these domains.
